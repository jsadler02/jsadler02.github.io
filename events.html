Upcoming Events

March 21, 2023


Come join us on March 21 as we invite members of NASA's Logistics Management Division to teach about the technology and equipment used to get to space! Our guests will be here March 21st from 11 A.M. to closing, so make sure to stop by!

April 28, 2023


On April 28, 2023, our satellite exhibit expands! From early models and prototypes to parts of retired satellites, our exhibit welcomes 8 new artifacts. These additions are permanent, so visit anytime on or after April 28 to learn more about these important pieces of space history!

June 2, 2023


Come join us June 2 as we invite members of NASA's Space Environment Testing Management Office to teach about what it takes to run NASA! Our guests will be here June 2nd from 12 P.M. to closing, so make sure to stop by!

July 16, 2023


Stop by on July 16 at 5 P.M. to watch the total solar eclipse! The museum will provide free saftey glasses along with a designated area to watch the eclipse. Before the eclipse, we will also have a presentation about eclipses, so that you know everything about them before one occurs. The next eclipse won't be for another 20 years, so don't miss it!
